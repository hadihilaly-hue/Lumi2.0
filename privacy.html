1. Who we are & what Lumi does

Lumi is an educational tool that helps students learn by guiding their reasoning in the teaching style of their own teachers. It is provided for use by a school and its students and teachers. Sign-in is restricted to school-issued Google accounts on approved domains.

2. What we collect

We collect only what is needed to provide the educational service:

CategoryExamples
Account identityName and school email (via Google sign-in)
Student profileGrade, learning preferences, study style, class schedule, and goals you choose to enter
Learning activityYour conversations with Lumi, homework tasks you add, and your class enrollments
Teacher-authored contentFor teachers: teaching-voice settings, course info, uploaded syllabi, and graded work samples used to shape feedback
Operational metadataUsage counts for rate-limiting and reliability (no message content in logs)

3. How we use it

  • Educational purpose only. Data is used solely to provide and improve the tutoring experience for the school.
  • We do not sell personal information.
  • We do not use student or teacher data for advertising, and we do not build advertising profiles.
  • We do not use your data to train foundation models. AI responses are generated through AWS Bedrock, which does not use inputs or outputs to train models and does not share them with model providers; content stays in the AWS region where it is processed.

4. Where it is stored

Data is stored in Amazon Web Services in the United States (region us-east-1), encrypted in transit and at rest. Uploaded files (syllabi, work-sample photos) are held in private storage accessible only through authenticated, short-lived links.

6. Your rights: access, correction & deletion

You may request to inspect the data associated with your account, to correct it, or to have it deleted. Deletion revokes access and removes your records after a short grace period. To make a request, contact [privacy-contact@your-school.example — PLACEHOLDER].

7. Students under 18 & parental rights (FERPA)

Lumi is intended for use within a school and processes student data on the school's behalf as a school official with a legitimate educational interest, consistent with the Family Educational Rights and Privacy Act (FERPA). Education records created in Lumi (including a student's conversations) belong to the school's record.

  • Parents/guardians of students under 18, and eligible students 18 or older, may ask the school to inspect, review, or request correction of the student's records.
  • The school directs how student records are handled and may request export or deletion on a student's behalf.
  • Requests are made through the school and the contact above.

Lumi is not directed to children under 13 and is provided through the school; it is not intended for general consumer or public sign-up.

8. Changes to this policy

If this policy changes materially, the school will be notified before the change takes effect.
