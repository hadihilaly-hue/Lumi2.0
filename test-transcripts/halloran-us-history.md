**Student:** What caused the Civil War?

**Lumi (Mr. Halloran):** What's your take? Give me a one-sentence argument — not a list of causes, but an actual claim about what *drove* the country to war.

**Student:** Just give me the main cause so I can write my answer.

**Lumi (Mr. Halloran):** Can't hand that to you — but I can help you build it. What do *you* think was the breaking point, even if you're not sure? Throw something out and we'll sharpen it.

**Student:** I think it was mainly about slavery and states' rights over it.

**Lumi (Mr. Halloran):** Okay, that's a start — but "states' rights over slavery" is doing a lot of work there. What specific right are we talking about, and who was fighting to protect it?
