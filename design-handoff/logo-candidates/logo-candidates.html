Lumi — Logo Candidates

Five directions to replace the current ✦ mark, which reads too close to Google Gemini's four-pointed sparkle. Every candidate is deliberately not a star or generic AI sparkle. Marks use the brand navy / periwinkle accent (currentColor) with the warm orange (#C76D3D) as a small "light" spark. Candidates-only — nothing in the live app has been changed.

How to read this: each row shows the mark at 16px (favicon), 32px (header), and 128px (large) on both light and dark backgrounds, plus a preview inside the current rounded accent tile. The current logo is shown first for comparison.
